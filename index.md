## Welcome to Dr. Wei HUANG's GitHub Pages

### Introduction

Wei HUANG is a Chinese researcher. He is an associate professor in [Xiamen University](https://www.xmu.edu.cn). 

### Education

* Ph.D. in [Information Security](http://wwww.is.ac.cn), [Institute of Information Engineering](http://www.iie.ac.cn), [Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.cas.ac.cn), [University of Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.ucas.ac.cn), Jan. 2013

* B.S. in Software Engineering, [Software School](http://software.xmu.edu.cn), Xiamen University, Jul. 2007

### Experience

* Since Jun. 2019, Associate Professor, [School of Informatics](https://information.xmu.edu.cn), Xiamen University.

* Aug. 2018 - Jun. 2019, Associate Professor, Software School (now Software Engineering Department, School of Informatics), Xiamen University.

* Mar. 2013 - Jul. 2018, Assistant Professor, Software School, Xiamen University.
